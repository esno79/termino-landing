Conversation
Context: A potential policyholder is discussing different types of insurance with an insurance agent.

Potential Policyholder: Hello! I’m looking to get some insurance, but I’m not sure what type I need.
Insurance Agent: Hi! I’d be happy to help. Are you interested in life insurance, health insurance, auto insurance, or property insurance?

Potential Policyholder: I think I need health insurance primarily. My medical expenses have been increasing.
Insurance Agent: That makes sense. We offer several health insurance plans that cover different medical services. Do you have a preference for a comprehensive plan or a more basic one?

Potential Policyholder: I’d prefer something comprehensive that covers preventive care as well.
Insurance Agent: Excellent choice! Additionally, have you considered life insurance? It’s important if you have dependents.

Potential Policyholder: I haven’t thought about it much. What type of life insurance would you recommend?
Insurance Agent: Term life insurance is often a good starting point because it’s more affordable and covers you for a specific period. Would you like more information on that?

Potential Policyholder: Yes, please! I’d like to understand the benefits and costs involved.
