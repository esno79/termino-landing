🧠 آموزش گرامر + تمرین

✅ حال کامل (Present Perfect)

  • ساختار: have/has + past participle
  • استفاده برای تجربیات، نتایج یا اتفاقات بدون زمان دقیق
  • مثال: Many people have realized the importance of life insurance.

✅ گذشته ساده (Past Simple)

  • ساختار: verb + ed یا شکل گذشته فعل
  • برای وقایع با زمان مشخص در گذشته
  • مثال: I purchased my first health insurance policy last year.

Present Perfect

  • No exact time
  • Effect/result important
  • Used with: already, ever, yet, just, since, for

Past Simple

  • Exact time known
  • Finished actions in the past
  • Used with: yesterday, last year, in 2020, two days ago
💡 نکته: اگر زمان برای شما مهم نیست و فقط اتفاق بودن مهم است → حال کامل. اگر زمان مشخص است → گذشته ساده.

📝 تمرین ۱: فعل مناسب را در جای خالی قرار دهید.

  • 1. Health insurance ___ (become) increasingly important due to rising medical costs.
  • 2. When someone ___ (pass) away, life insurance provides financial support to their family.
  • 3. Many drivers ___ (not understand) the terms of their auto insurance policy.
  • 4. The insurer ___ (offer) several different plans for homeowners.

📝 تمرین ۲: از گذشته ساده یا حال کامل استفاده کنید.

  • 1. I ___ (purchase) my first health insurance policy last year.
  • 2. Many people ___ (realize) the importance of life insurance after a family tragedy.
  • 3. The company ___ (introduce) a new auto insurance plan this month.
  • 4. Over the years, I ___ (review) my insurance needs multiple times.

✅ پاسخ‌ها

  1. has become
  2. passes
  3. do not understand
  4. offers
  5. purchased
  6. have realized
  7. has introduced
  8. have reviewed
