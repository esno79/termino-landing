Multiple-Choice Questions - Insurance❓

1. What is the primary purpose of life insurance?

  • a) To cover medical expenses
  • b) To provide financial security to beneficiaries upon the policyholder’s death ✅
  • c) To protect vehicles from accidents
  • d) To safeguard property from theft

2. Which of the following is typically included in auto insurance?

  • a) Property tax coverage
  • b) Collision coverage ✅
  • c) Business liability
  • d) Investment returns

3. What distinguishes whole life insurance from term life insurance?

  • a) It is mandatory by law
  • b) It only covers hospital stays
  • c) It accumulates cash value over time ✅
  • d) It is designed for renters

4. Health insurance plans can vary in what way?

  • a) They only cover dental services
  • b) They are the same worldwide
  • c) Some provide limited benefits, others offer comprehensive coverage ✅
  • d) All are employer-provided

5. Which statement is true about renters insurance?

  • a) It covers the building structure
  • b) It covers personal belongings in a rented property ✅
  • c) It is a form of health insurance
  • d) It replaces auto insurance

6. Why do insurers rely on the law of large numbers?

  • a) To increase premium prices
  • b) To improve accuracy in predicting losses ✅
  • c) To reduce customer satisfaction
  • d) To standardize all policies

✅ Answers

  1. Correct answer: b
  2. Correct answer: b
  3. Correct answer: c
  4. Correct answer: c
  5. Correct answer: b
  6. Correct answer: b
